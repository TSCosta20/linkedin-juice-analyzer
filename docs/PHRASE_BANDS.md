# Phrase Bands Reference

Scores are mapped to one of 10 bands:
- Band 0: score 0–9
- Band 1: score 10–19
- Band 2: score 20–29
- Band 3: score 30–39
- Band 4: score 40–49
- Band 5: score 50–59
- Band 6: score 60–69
- Band 7: score 70–79
- Band 8: score 80–89
- Band 9: score 90–100

